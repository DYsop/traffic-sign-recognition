key. Each entry provides a BibTeX-style citation anchor (the H3
heading, e.g. `### stallkamp2012man`), a full bibliographic entry in a
conventional style, and, where applicable, a DOI or permanent URL.

`[Stallkamp et al. (2012)](references.md#stallkamp2012man)`.
